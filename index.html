One Of Oldest and Best Institution

Kailali Vidya Niketan - Established by princple Topendra Shah in 2051. Kvn has been awarded as the best school of the Dhangadhi .Its graduates occupy admirable positions in medi­cine, engineering, banking and administration in Nepal as well as in abroad. KVN is renowned for career-oriented programs, personal education­al resources and top quality teaching faculties. It offers all the challeng­ing disciplines for higher education to its prospective students. KVN is leading institution in the country. It has maintained high educational reputation and offered the facilities to widen the horizons as well as to support the ambitions of the students. It has been the center of academic excellence which is always motivated to turn the students into dedicated, disciplined, efficient and innovative manpower for the nation.

Our facalitys

1. Experienced Faculty:
Our team of highly dedicated, experienced, and well-trained teachers are committed to providing the best education and support to every student.
2. Montessori-Based Education:
We offer Montessori-based education at the Pre-Primary level, ensuring a strong foundation for our youngest learners.
3. Affordable Fees:
We believe in making quality education accessible, which is why we offer affordable fee structures.
4. Nurturing Environment:
Our school provides a well-disciplined and homely environment, promoting both academic excellence and personal growth.
5. Outstanding Academic Results:
Our students consistently achieve outstanding SEE results, reflecting our commitment to academic excellence.
6. Child-Friendly Atmosphere:
We maintain a child-friendly school environment, ensuring that every student feels safe and supported.
7. Scholarship Opportunities:
We offer various scholarship facilities to support our students' educational journey.
8. Individualized Attention:
Each student receives individual care and attention, helping them to thrive academically and personally.
9. English Speaking Zone:
Our English-speaking zone helps students improve their language skills in a practical setting.
10. Extracurricular Activities:
We offer a range of extracurricular activities (ECA programmes) to foster all-round development.
11. Hostel Facilities:
We provide day and night hostel facilities for students who require accommodation.
12. Transportation:
Our transportation facilities ensure safe and convenient travel to and from school. We also provide facilities for games and sports.
13. Safety and Convenience:
Our campus is equipped with CCTV and generator facilities to ensure safety and uninterrupted learning.
14. Modern Labs:
We have state-of-the-art computer and science labs to support hands-on learning and exploration.
15. E-Learning:
Our audio-video e-learning classes integrate technology into education, making learning more interactive and engaging.
